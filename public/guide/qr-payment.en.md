# <strong>Q&A</strong>

---

## Q: Does QR API support an ordinary person?

<h2>A:</h2> <h4>No, due to the Bank of Thailand’s rules and regulations, this service only supports a juristic person.</h4>

---

## Q: What is the next step after completing the API test and submitting the application form?

<h2>A:</h2> <h4>KBank will verify the application form and reach to your registered phone no. or email. However, it may take some time to process because this will be the pilot phase to test the connection between the bank’s API and partner’s. We’re sorry about the inconvenience and trying our best to improve this.</h4>

---

## Q: Is there any cost for requesting QR API with KBank?

<h2>A:</h2> <h4>This process is free of charge for now. However, there might be some changes in the future.<h4>
